Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about finding, listing, and buying property on TagHouse.

How do I list a property on TagHouse?

Go to "List Property," fill in your property details (title, type, location, price), add descriptions, upload 2-5 high-quality images, and submit. Our team reviews your listing within 24 hours. Once approved, it goes live to thousands of buyers and renters.

Is it free to list a property?

Yes! Listing is completely free. There are no upfront costs. We only earn a commission when a transaction closes (2% for most property types). Our goal is to help you sell quickly at the best price.

How are agents verified on TagHouse?

Every agent undergoes a thorough verification process including identity verification, background checks, credential validation, and customer reference reviews. We display agent ratings, response times, and customer feedback so you can make informed decisions.

How do I schedule a property inspection?

Click "Request Inspection" on any listing. The agent receives your request immediately. Average response time is under 2 hours. You'll get a confirmation with the date, time, and agent's phone number. You can reschedule if needed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and mobile money (all major platforms). All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway. For large transactions, we can arrange wire transfers with proper documentation.

How is my money protected during transactions?

All transactions go through our secure escrow system. Your deposit is held safely by a neutral third party until both buyer and seller confirm the transaction is complete. Money is only released once all conditions are met. This protects everyone involved.

Can I edit or delete my listing?

Yes! You have complete control. Update details, prices, images, and descriptions anytime from your dashboard. Changes appear live within minutes. You can also unpublish or delete listings before a transaction is initiated. Contact support if you need help.

What if I need to cancel a listing?

You can cancel anytime if no transaction has been initiated. Simply go to your dashboard and select "Delete." If you have interested buyers, you might consider negotiating before canceling. If a transaction is in progress, contact our support team to discuss options.

How long does a typical property sale take?

From listing to completed sale typically takes 2-8 weeks depending on the property, location, and market conditions. Properties in high-demand areas sell faster. Well-priced properties with good photos tend to attract more inquiries. Our platform helps accelerate this process significantly.

Do you help with contracts and legal documents?

We provide standard contract templates and can recommend vetted legal professionals in your area. However, we strongly recommend hiring a lawyer to review any agreement before signing. TagHouse is not a legal service, but we connect you with professionals who can help.

Is my personal data secure and private?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption for all data transmission. Your personal information is never shared with third parties without your consent. We comply with Nigerian data protection regulations and international privacy standards. Read our Privacy Policy for complete details.

How do I contact TagHouse support?

Reach us 24/7 via phone (+234 0123 456 7890), email (support@taghouse.com), or WhatsApp. During business hours (Mon-Sat, 8am-6pm WAT), our team responds within 1-2 hours. For urgent issues, call our emergency line. We're here to help!

What makes TagHouse different from other platforms?

We focus on transparency, security, and speed. Every agent is verified. Every listing is checked. Every transaction is protected. Our average response time is under 2 hours (industry average is 24+ hours). We're committed to making real estate transactions fast, fair, and friction-free.

Can I save my favorite properties?

Yes! Create a free account and save properties to your "Shortlist." You can organize favorites by type, location, or price range. Compare properties side-by-side. You'll get notifications when similar properties are listed and when prices change.

How do I become a verified agent on TagHouse?

Visit our Careers page or email agents@taghouse.com to inquire about agent partnerships. We look for experienced professionals with proven track records. Agents get access to our platform, marketing tools, and lead generation system. Commission splits are competitive and transparent.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. We believe in transparent pricing. Listing is free. Our commission is clear upfront (2% on sale, negotiable on rentals). No surprises. No hidden charges. Everything is explained during onboarding. If you have questions about fees, ask before proceeding.
